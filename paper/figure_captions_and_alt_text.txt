Figure 1. Embedding pipeline.
Alt text: A flowchart partitions a binary cover into 3-by-3 blocks, identifies
frequent and absent patterns, ranks distance-one substitutions with a CNN,
selects a net-capacity-maximizing mapping prefix, embeds the payload, and
serializes the auxiliary stream.

Figure 2. Extraction and restoration pipeline.
Alt text: A flowchart decodes the auxiliary stream, constructs a globally
injective inverse dictionary, scans stego blocks, extracts the declared number
of bits, and restores the original cover.

Figure 3. CNN architecture.
Alt text: Three 9-by-9 input channels pass through convolution layers with 16
and 32 channels, adaptive average pooling, and a two-layer scalar regressor.

Figure 4. Binary document benchmark.
Alt text: Eight labeled binary images show a circuit, formula, text, graph,
handwriting, a large glyph, symbols, and a table.

Figure 5. Payload scaling.
Alt text: Two line charts compare mean net payload and median DRD for ABM,
Dong, Huynh--Nguyen, and PPOCP from 64 to 512 target bits.

Figure 6. Availability and net-bits--DRD trajectories.
Alt text: A grouped bar chart reports availability, and a line chart plots
mean net payload against median DRD for four payload targets.

Figure 7. Binarization strata.
Alt text: Two line charts compare ABM availability and mean net payload for
images classified as suitable or unsuitable for fixed-threshold binarization.

Figure 8. Resource profile.
Alt text: Two bar charts compare median runtime and incremental peak memory
for ABM, Dong, Huynh--Nguyen, and PPOCP at a 256-bit target.

Figure 9. Steganalysis.
Alt text: Two line charts report grouped ROC AUC across payloads for logistic
and Random Forest detectors; a dashed horizontal line marks chance level.
